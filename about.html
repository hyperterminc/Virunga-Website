About Us

24 Years of Excellence • Connecting Rwanda's Northern & Western Regions Since 2001

Our Services Get Started

+20 Years of Trusted Service

VIRUNGA Express has been Rwanda's leading transport company, proudly serving the Northern, Southern and Western regions with unwavering commitment to excellence. Named after the majestic Virunga Mountains that define our landscape, we embody the same strength and reliability that characterizes our beautiful region.

Our extensive network connects major cities to remote communities, navigating challenging terrains and diverse weather conditions with expertise gained through 15 years of dedicated service. Whether you're traveling for business, visiting family, or shipping cargo, our proven track record of excellence ensures you reach your destination safely and on time.

From bustling urban centers to remote villages nestled in the hills, VIRUNGA Express bridges distances and connects communities, making us an integral part of Rwanda's transportation infrastructure.
